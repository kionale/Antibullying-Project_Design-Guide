Colours
& Theme

It is important to consider the emotional responses that colors can evoke when selecting a color palette for an app aimed at educating users on empathy, personal growth, and resilience in the face of bullying. Colors that are calming and energizing can create a sense of compassion, wisdom, and peace. Similarly, colors that promote positivity and growth can encourage and create a safe and welcoming environment. Selecting the right color palette is critical in effectively conveying the intended message and resonating with the audience.

Primary Background Color

Seashell
HEX #FFF8F4
R 255 G 248 B 244

Seashell is a contemporary and versatile color that brings tranquility and relaxation to any design, promoting feelings of calmness and serenity. It symbolizes reliability and durability, inspiring trust and confidence. As we design our anti-bullying app, it is crucial to choose a color palette that is vibrant yet not overwhelming. Seashell's gentle and understated nature makes it an excellent background color, creating a welcoming and harmonious environment for users.

Primary Theme Color

Chinese Violet
HEX #896686
R 137 G 102 B 134

Chinese Violet combines blue's calming stability with red's fierce energy, making it associated with positive traits such as compassion, wisdom, and independence. It symbolizes achievements and ambition, all while Its calming properties also make it a symbol of peace. By incorporating Chinese Violet into our brand identity, we can inspire others to embrace these qualities and work towards creating a better world.

Ash Grey
HEX #B1BFA9
R 177 G 191 B 169

Ash Grey symbolizes hope, resilience, and balance, reminding us of growth and renewal amidst challenges. It reduces stress and promotes well-being, making it ideal to promote calm in the face of bullying. Ash Grey aids in healing and represents personal development, empowerment, and progress. It encourages individuals to cultivate their strengths and strive towards their goals with confidence.
