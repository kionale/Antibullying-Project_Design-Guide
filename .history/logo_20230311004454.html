About
Gotchu

Gotchu was named to evoke a sense of understanding and protection for individuals who are experiencing or witnessing bullying, implying a commitment to being there for someone in a particular situation. The name is catchy and easy to remember, making it a perfect fit for our app, and we hope to create a sense of trust and community within our app by expressing our commitment to support and protect our users. At Gotchu, our innovative anti-bullying app aims to empower individuals to take a stand against bullying and promote kindness and empathy. We create a safe and supportive environment for users to explore emotions and receive advice, and users can learn about the effects of bullying on mental health, self-esteem, and relationships. Our app is accessible to everyone and available in multiple languages. Every likely individual is welcome to join our community of supportive individuals committed to ending bullying and promoting kindness and empathy.
