Logo
& Usage

Our logo, featuring Hugo embracing the letter 'O', draws inspiration from the powerful gesture of a sympathetic hug. This is significant in Western cultures where the 'O' in 'XO' represents a hug. To enhance the emotional aspect of the design, Hugo's closed eyes were added. The incorporation of the letters 'G' and 'C' strengthens our Gotchu brand identity. The versatility of Hugo allows for easy adaptation to various colors and objects while maintaining its impact on transparent backgrounds.
